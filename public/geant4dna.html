ESTAR reference: NIST Physical Reference Data, liquid water (ρ=1 g/cm³). Geant4-DNA's Emfietzoglou model is known to deviate from ESTAR at E < 1 keV (Emfietzoglou is tuned for liquid water, ESTAR is vapor-based Bethe-Bloch). A ratio of 1.0±0.1 above 3 keV is "passing". Below 1 keV, agreement within ~2× is expected — real validation should compare to Geant4-DNA output directly.
