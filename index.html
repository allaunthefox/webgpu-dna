WebGPU Geant4-DNA modular TypeScript build

Modular TS port of the monolithic harness. Runs mono-energetic primary electrons across 8 ESTAR energies, measures CSDA & stopping power, runs Karamitros IRT chemistry at 10 keV, and scores SSB/DSB on a 21×21 B-DNA fiber grid.

ESTAR reference: NIST Physical Reference Data, liquid water (ρ=1 g/cm³). Geant4-DNA's Emfietzoglou model deviates from ESTAR at E < 1 keV. A ratio of 1.0±0.1 above 3 keV is "passing". Below 1 keV, agreement within ~2× is expected.
